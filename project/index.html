    • Introduction

    Edemia as we know it began on October 13th, 2016. During a brainstorming session hosted on Skype and a shared Google Docs,
    we came up with the name of our new world: Edemia.

    In truth, Edemia had been in the works for over a year before this moment, only under a different name. It all started with the creation of a fantastical species called Usogami. Originally they were just a fan made Pok√©mon species that we'd inserted into our stories. But soon our development of them grew, and they needed their own world.

    Now eight years later, Edemia has come a long way from a few words in a google document. With 800+ named and designed characters, and well over 100,000 words written - we've been busy! Edemia is now home to 6+ sapient species, 13+ nations, and a diverse pantheon of gods. All of this wouldn't have been possible without the dedicated work of so many.
    You can find some of them below!

    Magixin (Lolly)

    The OG

    Lolly is where Edemia all started. The original creator of Usogami (or Usogameon as they were originally called) Lolly roped us all in and made us fall in love with Uso. It's because of her that we all originally met and started working on this amazing project.

    The Uso Queen

    Ren has often been the driving force in our worldbuilding and the fleshing out of characters, timelines, and cultures. They have a sweet spirit and have served as a warm figure of authority in our brainstorming. Their creativity and artwork is incredible and has been invaluable in Edemia's development.

    Trio has been invaluable in keeping us organized and collecting information. They recently took on the incredible task of compiling all of our timelines into one, and are responsible for most of our spreadsheets keeping track of characters, events, dates, and more!
