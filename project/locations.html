    The world of Edemia is a magical and varied place.
    A little smaller than our own Earth, Edemia is home to several different landmasses and hundreds of cultures. The main two continents are known as Lassi and Overgrowth. But there are several other islands and smaller continents, such as Equalis and Aurevant.

    For the purposes of this page Nokfill and Phurull are grouped with Overgrowth, but the groupings are a little arbitrary. The map of Edemia has undergone a lot of tweaking over the years, and the current map only distinguishes general polictical/cultural borders. It's also missing a map of the many sky islands that litter Edemia.
