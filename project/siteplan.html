Purpose

The purpose of this site is to provide a brief overview of the fantastical setting of Edemia. A magical world that my friends and I have worked on for almost 10 years.

My target audience is anyone that doesn't know, or would like to know more about Edemia! It will cover the species unique of the world, and different locations in the world, as well as a brief interactive trivia section.

Questions about the formation of Edemia like "Who is involed with Edemia?" or "When did you get started?" will be answered on the home page, including links to the social media of those involved in its creation and development.
