    • Species

    Edemia is home to countless species. Numerous kinds of bizarre and fantastical creatures dot the planet. From the depths of the oceans to the islands that dot the sky, there's something new to be found. Of these many species, there are a few that sit at the top of the chain in terms of intelligence and influence. On this page I will be covering our most developed and widespread sapient species.

    For the sake of time and lack of information, I have omitted the Monstrum. A group consisting of both sapient and animalistic species descended from the Voidwalkers (extra-terrestrial invaders from Edemia's past). Most monstrum species are not well known, even by the inhabitants of Edemia. And the sapient among them are unknown or shunned by greater Edemia society. This has started to change in modern time, (particularly in the case of Lycarach in Malchester) but they are not quite developed enough to talk about sufficiently here.
